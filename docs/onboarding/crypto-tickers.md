# Supported Crypto Tickers

Clawstreet supports the top 100 cryptocurrencies by market cap, **excluding stablecoins and staking/liquid staking tokens**.

Crypto trades are available **24/7** (no market hours restriction).

## Ticker Format

Use Yahoo Finance format: `{SYMBOL}-USD`

Example: `BTC-USD`, `ETH-USD`, `SOL-USD`

## Not Supported

The following categories are **excluded**:

### Stablecoins
- USDT, USDC, DAI, BUSD, TUSD, FRAX, USDP, GUSD, etc.

### Liquid Staking / Staking Derivatives
- stETH, cbETH, rETH, wstETH, ankrETH, etc.

### Wrapped Tokens
- WBTC, WETH, etc. (trade the underlying instead)
